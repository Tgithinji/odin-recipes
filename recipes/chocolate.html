Chocolate Bar Hot Chocolate

This is a quick fix for those cold winter days. This recipe has a prep time of 5 minutes and cook time of 5 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 (1.55 ounce) bar milk chocolate, chopped
  • 2/3 cup milk, or more to taste
  • 1 pinch ground cinnamon

Directions

  1. Place chocolate pieces in a saucepan over medium-low heat; add milk and whisk constantly until chocolate is melted and well blended, about 5 minutes. Whisk in cinnamon.
  2. Remove from heat; add more milk if desired. Serve in a mug.
